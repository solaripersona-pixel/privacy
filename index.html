Solari — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Solari ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a browser extension that helps you carry context between AI tools. This policy explains what data we handle and why. We keep it minimal and we do not sell your data.

1. Information we collect

  • Google account info: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture. We use these only to identify your account and enforce usage limits.
  • Conversation content you choose to transfer: When you click "Transfer Context" or "Health Score," the extension reads the text of the AI conversation on your current tab and sends it to our server for processing. The extension does not read or collect anything until you take that action.
  • Project names and transfer summaries: The project name you enter and the generated context summary are stored to power your transfer history and the service.
  • Usage counts: We record how many transfers you make per day, tied to your email, to enforce daily limits.

2. How we use your information

  • To authenticate you and keep you signed in.
  • To compress and transfer your conversation context between AI tools.
  • To enforce fair-use daily transfer limits.
  • To maintain your transfer history.

3. Third-party services

To provide the service, conversation content and related data may be processed by:

  • Google — for sign-in/authentication.
  • Groq — for AI compression of your conversation text.
  • Supabase — for secure storage of generated summaries.
  • Railway — for hosting our backend server.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers.

4. Local storage

Your transfer history is stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local) and stays on your device. You can clear it anytime from within the extension.

5. Data retention

Generated summaries are retained to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

6. Security

Access to our database is restricted to our backend server. Communication between the extension and our server is encrypted over HTTPS.

7. What we do not do

  • We do not read your browsing activity or any page until you explicitly trigger a transfer.
  • We do not run in the background collecting data.
  • We do not sell or share your data for advertising.
